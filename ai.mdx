## Getting started

1. **Enable llms.txt** - Mintlify automatically generates this file for better AI indexing
2. **Add contextual menu** - Configure one-click AI integrations in your `docs.json`
3. **Set up MCP server** - Connect your documentation to AI development tools
4. **Optimize content** - Use clear headings and structured content for better AI understanding

Your documentation becomes more valuable when it's easily accessible to both human readers and AI tools. These features work together to create a seamless experience for users working with AI assistants.
